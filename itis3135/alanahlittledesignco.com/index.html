Services

Alanah Little Design Co. builds modern, user-centered websites for students, creators, and small businesses. I combine thoughtful UX with solid front-end development so your site looks great, loads fast, and is easy to maintain.

What makes my firm different:

  • Accessibility-first structure using clean semantic HTML.
  • CRAP-based layouts that feel organized and intentional.
  • Mobile-friendly design that still looks good on desktop.
  • Reusable components (headers/footers) to keep pages consistent.
  • Readable CSS with simple, maintainable styling choices.
  • UX-focused decisions: clarity, hierarchy, and scannability.
  • Performance-minded assets and lightweight styling.
  • Professional polish: spacing, alignment, and typography that “click.”

For clients, I can design and develop a multi-page website, improve an existing layout, clean up HTML/CSS for readability, and create a consistent visual system (colors, typography, spacing) that matches your brand. I also provide basic guidance on content structure so your site communicates clearly and feels easy to navigate.
