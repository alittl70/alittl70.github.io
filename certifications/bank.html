Expedition Fund Manager

Every great adventure requires careful resource management. The Expedition Fund Manager keeps track of your safari budget so you can focus on the journey, not the spreadsheets.

Deposit funds before each leg of the expedition, withdraw what you need for supplies and gear, and check your balance at any time. Every transaction is logged so you always know exactly where your resources went — whether it was binoculars in Nairobi or a jeep rental in the Maasai Mara.

Smart lions don't just hunt — they plan. Keep your expedition financially sound with every step of the journey accounted for.

Built with JavaScript classes and object-oriented programming as part of the FCC JavaScript Certification.
