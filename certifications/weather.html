Safari Weather Watch

A seasoned explorer never heads into the bush without checking the skies. The Safari Weather Watch pulls live weather data from cities around the world so you can plan your next expedition with confidence.

Select a destination from the dropdown — New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Paris, Tokyo, or London — and instantly see current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and sky conditions. Because whether you're tracking lions in the heat or spotting leopards in the rain, the weather always matters.

Know before you go. The savanna rewards the prepared.

Built with JavaScript async/await and the OpenWeather API as part of the FCC JavaScript Certification.
