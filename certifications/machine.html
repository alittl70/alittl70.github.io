Jungle Rhythm Machine

The savanna is never silent. From the thundering hooves of a wildebeest migration to the distant rumble of a lion's roar, the jungle pulses with rhythm around the clock.

The Jungle Rhythm Machine lets you tap into that energy. Each pad on the board is mapped to a sound inspired by the wild — trigger them with your keyboard or a click, and build your own safari soundscape one beat at a time.

Press Q, W, E, A, S, D, Z, X, or C to unleash the sounds of the bush. Stack them, layer them, and find your rhythm in the wild.

Built with JavaScript event listeners and audio elements as part of the FCC JavaScript Certification.
