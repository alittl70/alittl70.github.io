Field Note Converter

Every great safari begins with careful documentation. The Field Note Converter is a tool built for adventurers who need to transform their raw expedition notes into polished, structured reports.

Using simple markdown syntax, explorers can format their field observations — from sighting headers to bold species names and italicized habitat notes — and instantly see them rendered into clean HTML, ready to share with the pride back home.

Whether you're logging a lion sighting at dawn or cataloguing the sounds of the savanna at dusk, the Field Note Converter keeps your documentation as sharp as your instincts.

Built with JavaScript and regular expressions as part of the FCC JavaScript Certification.
