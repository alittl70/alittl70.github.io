Evan Capitain — Charlotte, NC

Background

A photographer with a passion for authentic moments

Evan Capitain is a Charlotte-based freelance photographer with over five years of experience capturing portraits, events, landscapes, and commercial imagery across the Carolinas. He began shooting professionally in 2019 and has since built a reputation for his calm presence on set and his ability to put subjects at ease — resulting in images that feel natural, not posed.

His approach blends documentary instinct with careful composition. Whether he's working with golden-hour light for an outdoor portrait session or navigating the fast-paced energy of a live event, Evan prioritizes the authentic moment over the perfect setup.

He has contributed work to regional publications and collaborated with local businesses, restaurants, and event venues on commercial projects throughout the Charlotte metro area.
